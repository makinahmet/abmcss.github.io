HERO SECTIONS
   Hero sections are the areas that immediately shows up on the screen under your logo and menu. Your hero should clearly explain who you are, what you do, and the value of your product or service. If your visitor can't figure out what you offer within a few seconds, they'll likely abandon your site as quickly as they arrived. They need to understand–in an instant–exactly what you bring to the table.
